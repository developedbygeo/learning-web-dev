Diablo 4

What is Diablo 4

Diablo IV is a 2023 online-only action role-playing dungeon crawling game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. It is the fourth main installment in the Diablo series.

Gameplay

Classes

In Diablo you choose among five classes

Barbarian
A medium health with good damage class
Druid
A high health and low damage class
Necromancer
A low health class using people they kill to fight for them
Sorcerer
A very low health class but with insane ranged damage
